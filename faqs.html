Frequently Asked Questions

What types of books are available at LibraryHub?

We offer a wide range of books, including fiction, non-fiction, academic, research materials, and children's books. You can explore our collection based on your reading needs.

How can I borrow a book?

Borrowing a book is simple! Just visit our library, choose your desired book, and complete the borrowing process at the front desk. You can also check book availability online through our catalog.

What are the library's operating hours?

Our library is open from 9 AM to 8 PM on weekdays and 10 AM to 6 PM on weekends. Special hours may apply during holidays.
